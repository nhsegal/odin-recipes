Sumac Chicken

Description

Apparently this is a Palestinian dish. It goes great with a flat bread like pita and typical Mediterranean sides like hummus, olives, and tomato-cucumber salad. Sumac is available by mail-order if you can't find it in your supermarket. Top with yogurt sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 pieces of chicken
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 red onion, coarsely sliced
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons sumac
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a bowl combine the oil, garlic, sumac, lemon juice, salt and pepper.
  2. Coat the chicken with this mixture, add the onion, and let it marinade >4 hours in the fridge. (24 hrs is fine.)
  3. Preheat the oven to 200 Celsius.
  4. Spread out the marinaded chicken and onions on a sheetpan lined with baking paper or foil and bake for 25 minutes in the oven.
